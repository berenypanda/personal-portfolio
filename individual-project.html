About this project

Realizing how often websites redesign their pages to look more modern, I decided to practice this skill with an old website of mine. I redesigned my old blog I had in my high school journalism class, focusing on creating responsive design from what I learned in my classes as well as creating a design that felt more current.

Responsive Design

I tried to come up with layouts that would suit the mobile, tablet, and desktop experience.

Creating Components and Variants through Figma

I made reusuable components for the button and navigation that could be toggled to match the appropriate screen size.

Web Design

Drawing inspiration from my old blog as well as other blogs, I learned how to choose a color scheme that felt sleek but also captured the mood of my old blog.
